GenAI Education Taskforce at Discover

AI Leadership Education Enterprise

Project Overview

As the Co-chair of the GenAI Education Taskforce at Discover Financial Services, I led the initiative to educate and implement Generative AI technologies across the enterprise. This project was crucial in fostering innovation and improving efficiency throughout the organization.

Challenges

  • Introducing complex AI concepts to a diverse workforce
  • Ensuring alignment across various departments including cybersecurity, data science, engineering, and business functions
  • Balancing innovation with regulatory compliance and data security

Solutions

  • Developed a comprehensive education program tailored to different roles and departments
  • Created a cross-functional network to facilitate knowledge sharing and collaboration
  • Implemented a phased approach to GenAI adoption, starting with low-risk, high-impact areas

Results

  • Increased awareness and understanding of GenAI technologies across the organization
  • Successful implementation of GenAI solutions in key business processes, resulting in improved efficiency and customer experience
  • Established Discover as a leader in AI adoption within the financial services industry

Key Learnings

This project highlighted the importance of cross-functional collaboration and the need for continuous education in rapidly evolving technological fields. It also reinforced the value of balancing innovation with responsible AI practices.
